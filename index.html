I am an undergraduate student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) Singapore, pursuing a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering with specialization in Robotics and Mechatronics Stream. With a CGPA of 4.64/5.00 and top 5% First Class Honor, I am passionate about robotics research and control systems.

My research interests focus on Compliance Force Control in Manipulation and Locomotion for quadruped and bipedal robots. I work on electromechanical modeling of series-elastic actuators, impedance control strategies, and developing robust control systems for challenging terrain navigation.

Projects ü¶æ

Series-Elastic Actuator Electromechanical Modeling
  • Research on electromechanical model of Series-Elastic Actuator using HEBI motors
  • Designed experiments to estimate motor parameters through MATLAB API
  • Discovered discrepancies between estimated parameters and datasheet values
  • Analyzed experimental errors, motor aging phenomena, and related factors
Impedance Control for Robotic Arms
  • Built planar dual-arm and 6-DOF robotic arm using HEBI motors
  • Implemented impedance control with spring-damper system attached to end-effector
  • Characterized end-point position changes using joystick inputs via Teleoperation
  • Mapped Cartesian forces to joint torques using Jacobian transformation
Quadruped Robot Jumping Simulation
  • Implemented virtual spring-damper system between hip and foot of robot legs
  • Enabled upward jumping motion through Gazebo simulations using Unitree guide
  • Studied optimal control and sim-to-real gap for real-world implementation
  • Research inspired by "Quadruped-Frog: Rapid Online Optimization of Continuous Quadruped Jumping"
