I am a graduate student focusing on robotics and automation, with particular interest in multi-contact manipulation and robust control systems. My research aims to develop stable and reliable robotic systems that can operate effectively in complex, real-world environments.

My current research focuses on stable robust multi-contact manipulation, where I develop planning frameworks that enable robots to perform complex manipulation tasks through multiple contact points while maintaining stability and robustness under various uncertainties.
